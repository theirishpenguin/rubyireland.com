Welcome to Ruby Ireland
What is Ruby Ireland?

A great language deserves a great community - Ruby Ireland is a melting pot teeming with Ruby enthusiasts the length and breath of Ireland. We share a passion for the Ruby language, the frameworks it's spawned such as Rails and collaborating with one another to share ideas code and fun.
When do you meetup?

Ruby Tuesday is where it's at. Usually the group meets on the first Tuesday of every month in the Trinity Capital hotel on Pearse St., Dublin. There is also a newly started monthly Cork meetup with details of each meetup up and coming meetup posted here.

Should I got to the meetup?

Absolutely! Whether you're new to the game or an old hand at Ruby hacking, the meetups are there for everyone to attend and the lifeblood of any community. Newbie's are especially welcome - bring along your questions and we'll try to help out. There's usually a presentation or two at each meetup. The food and venue are proudly sponsored by Jolt Gaming Online - check them out at www.joltonline.com.

Not everyone is near a meetup or can attend one. The easiest way to keep in contact is through the Mailing List. There is also a little activity on the IRC channel at #ruby.ie on irc.freenode.net. Join in and help make community even better!
